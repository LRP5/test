Chicken Tikka Masala

- Creamy, Spicy, Delicious.




Ingredients:
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

  • 1 cup plain yogurt (Greek yogurt works well)

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander

  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika

  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric

  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Juice of 1 lemon

  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish (optional)


    • Instructions:
  • Marinate the Chicken:

    • In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, olive oil, minced garlic, minced ginger, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, turmeric, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together until well combined.

  • Preheat the Oven:

    • Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).

  • Bake the Chicken:

    • Remove the marinated chicken from the refrigerator and place it in a baking dish.

    Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C). Cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts, so it's a good idea to use a meat thermometer to check for doneness.

  • Broil for a Crispy Finish (Optional):
    •

    If you'd like to give the chicken a slightly crispy finish, you can turn on the broiler for the last 2-3 minutes of cooking. Keep an eye on it to prevent burning.

  • Serve:
    •

    Once the chicken is cooked through and has a nice golden color, remove it from the oven.

    Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the chicken breasts for a burst of flavor.

    Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.




    This Chicken Tikka Masala recipe is my absolute favorite because it combines the flavors of Indian spices and creamy yogurt to create a rich and savory dish. The chicken turns out tender, and the marinade adds a delightful depth of flavor. Plus, it's easy to make!
